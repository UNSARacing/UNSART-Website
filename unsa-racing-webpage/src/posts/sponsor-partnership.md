# New Partnership with Leading Engineering Software

**Published:** November 10, 2024  
**Author:** Sponsorship Team

---

We are excited to announce our strategic partnerships with industry-leading engineering software providers. These partnerships will significantly enhance our design and analysis capabilities.

## Our Partners

### SolidWorks
SolidWorks provides us with professional CAD software, enabling our design team to create detailed 3D models and technical drawings with precision and efficiency.

### ANSYS
Through ANSYS, we have access to powerful simulation tools for structural analysis, fluid dynamics, and thermal analysis. This allows us to validate our designs before manufacturing.

### MATLAB
MATLAB and Simulink help us with data analysis, control systems design, and vehicle dynamics simulation. These tools are essential for optimizing our car's performance.

### Racing Arena
Racing Arena supports us with racing expertise and industry connections, helping bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world motorsports.

## Impact on Our Project

These partnerships enable us to:

- **Design** with industry-standard tools
- **Simulate** and validate designs before building
- **Analyze** performance data effectively
- **Learn** professional engineering workflows

## Looking Forward

We're grateful for the support of our sponsors and look forward to showcasing what's possible when students have access to professional-grade tools and resources.

If your organization is interested in supporting our team, please reach out to us at info@unsaracing.com.
