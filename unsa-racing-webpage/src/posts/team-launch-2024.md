# UNSA Racing Team Launches in 2024

**Published:** September 15, 2024  
**Author:** Team Management

---

We are thrilled to announce the official launch of the **UNSA Racing Team**, marking a significant milestone in our journey toward Formula Student competition excellence.

## Our Mission

Founded in 2024 at the University of Sarajevo, our team consists of passionate students driven by a shared enthusiasm for motorsports and automotive innovation. Our participation in the Formula Student competition represents a commitment to applying theoretical knowledge to real-world engineering challenges.

## What's Next?

Over the coming months, we will be:

- **Designing** our first Formula Student race car
- **Building** partnerships with industry sponsors
- **Recruiting** talented students from various engineering disciplines
- **Preparing** for our first competition season

## Join Us

If you're a student at the University of Sarajevo and share our passion for automotive engineering, we'd love to have you on the team. Contact us to learn more about available positions and how you can contribute.
